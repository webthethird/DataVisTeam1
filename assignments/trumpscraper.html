TALLER THAN

TRUMP

Taller than Trump visualizes corporate branding of skyscrapers and other large urban structures around the world, using President Donald Trump's prominent global brand as a benchmark for measuring the prevalence and influence of transnational investments of capital in the urban environment. We used data and imagery collected from SkyscraperPage.com to sort buildings from cities around the world according to major developers and owners (Trump has 25 structures in the US, Canada, India, Panama, South Korea, Philippines, and Turkey). Elevation images of the buildings show the prominence of Trumpâ€™s brand in cities around the world. By comparing other major global brands to the controversial name of Donald Trump, the project reveals the political and economic forces driving the construction of the built environment, and their effects on skylines.
